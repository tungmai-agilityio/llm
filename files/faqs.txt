Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order by clicking on the "Track Order" link in the order confirmation email or by logging into your account on our website and navigating to the "Orders" section.

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after it's been placed?
A: You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team. After that, cancellations or modifications may not be possible, depending on the order status.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: Our return policy varies by product category. Generally, you can return most items within 30-60 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please refer to the "Returns and Refunds" section for more details.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping services?
A: Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee during the checkout process.

Q: What is Amazon Prime?
A: Amazon Prime is a membership program that offers fast, free delivery on eligible items, exclusive access to movies, TV shows, ad-free music, Kindle books, and more.

Q: How do I return an item?
A: To return an item, go to "Your Orders" in your account, select the item you want to return, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the return process.

Q: What payment methods does Amazon accept?
A: Amazon accepts major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. You can also use Amazon gift cards and Amazon Pay for purchases.

Q: How can I cancel or change my order?
A: You can cancel or modify an order before it enters the shipping process. Go to "Your Orders" and select the order to cancel or make changes.

Q: What are Amazon's shipping options?
A: Amazon offers various shipping options, including Standard Shipping, Two-Day Shipping, and Same-Day Delivery for Prime members. Shipping options vary depending on the item and location.

Q: How do I contact Amazon customer service?
A: You can contact Amazon customer service through the "Help" section on the website. You can chat with a representative or request a call to assist with your inquiry.

Q: What is Amazon's refund policy?
A: Amazon typically issues refunds once the returned item has been received. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 3-5 business days.

Q: How do I change my account information?
A: To update your account details, go to "Your Account" and select the section you want to edit, such as your address, payment methods, or password.

Q: Does Amazon offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes, Amazon offers gift-wrapping services for eligible items. You can select this option during checkout for an additional fee.

Q: What is Subscribe & Save?
A: Subscribe & Save is a program that allows you to set up automatic deliveries for eligible items and receive discounts on each subscription order.

Q: How do I use an Amazon gift card?
A: To use an Amazon gift card, enter the gift card code during checkout. The card balance will be applied to your order total.

Q: Can I use Amazon internationally?
A: Yes, Amazon ships to many international destinations, although some products may not be available for shipping outside of the U.S. Check product availability and international shipping options during checkout.

Q: What is the Amazon Marketplace?
A: The Amazon Marketplace is a platform where third-party sellers can list and sell products. You can shop for items sold directly by Amazon or from independent sellers.

Q: How do I leave feedback for a seller?
A: After your order is delivered, you can leave feedback by going to "Your Orders," selecting the product, and clicking "Leave Seller Feedback."

Q: What are Amazon Coupons?
A: Amazon Coupons provide discounts on selected products. You can apply the coupon during checkout to get instant savings.

Q: What is Amazon Fresh?
A: Amazon Fresh is a grocery delivery service offering fresh produce, dairy, and other household essentials delivered to your door. It is available in select areas.

Q: How do I qualify for free shipping on Amazon?
A: Free shipping is available for orders over a certain amount or for Amazon Prime members on eligible items. The minimum order amount varies by location.

Q: What is Amazon's policy on counterfeit items?
A: Amazon has a strict policy against counterfeit products. If you receive a counterfeit item, contact customer service to report the issue and request a refund or replacement.

Q: How do I subscribe to Amazon Prime Video?
A: To subscribe to Prime Video, you can join Amazon Prime, which includes access to Prime Video as part of your membership. You can stream thousands of movies and TV shows.
