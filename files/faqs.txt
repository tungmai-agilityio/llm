Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order by clicking on the "Track Order" link in the order confirmation email or by logging into your account on our website and navigating to the "Orders" section.

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after it's been placed?
A: You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team. After that, cancellations or modifications may not be possible, depending on the order status.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: Our return policy varies by product category. Generally, you can return most items within 30-60 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please refer to the "Returns and Refunds" section for more details.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping services?
A: Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee during the checkout process.
